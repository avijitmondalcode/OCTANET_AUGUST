About Hospital

A hospital is a medical facility that provides a range of healthcare services, including diagnosis, treatment, and care for patients who are ill or injured. Hospitals can vary in size and specialty, and they often have different departments and units to address various medical needs. Here are some key points about hospitals: Healthcare Professionals: Hospitals employ a variety of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, surgeons, radiologists, laboratory technicians, pharmacists, and administrative staff.
